## Safety

Laser cutters and engravers are dangerous tools. Everything this project produces —
material settings, generated `.xcs` files, project designs, troubleshooting steps —
is **guidance, not a guarantee**.

- **Always run a test cut/engrave on scrap** before committing to a real job.
- **Never cut PVC, vinyl, or any chlorine-containing material.** It releases
  chlorine gas that is toxic to you and corrosive to your machine.
- **Never run a laser unattended.** Keep a fire extinguisher within reach.
- **Use proper ventilation / fume extraction**, and manufacturer-approved eye protection.
- Follow the safety instructions in your machine's official documentation. When this
  project and xTool's official guidance disagree, **follow xTool.**

Material settings vary by machine revision, laser module, lens, material batch,
ambient conditions, and firmware. Numbers surfaced by this project are starting
points sourced from public documentation and the community, each carrying a
confidence level and provenance. You are responsible for safe operation.
